About Us

In a world filled with challenges and disparities, it is our duty to step forward and make a difference.

"DO BETTER BE BETTER IN THE SOCIETY" is a non profit organization dedicated to providing support and assistance to vulnerable populations, homeless individuals, and orphaned children in our community.

Our Missions

Our mission is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves access to basic necessities, care, and opportunities that can help them lead fulfilling lives.

Our Main Objectives

Supporting Vulnerable Populations

We aim to provide shelter, food, clothing, and essential resources to those in need, including homeless individuals and families facing crisis situations.

Empowering Orphaned Children

We strive to create a safe and nurturing environment for orphaned children, offering education, healthcare, and emotional support to help them build a better future.

Community Outreach and Advocacy

Through awareness campaigns, partnerships with local organizations, and advocacy efforts, we seek to address the root causes of vulnerability in our community and promote systemic change for a more inclusive society.

Programs & Initiatives

Emergency Shelter and Relief Services

Providing temporary shelter, food, and emergency assistance
to homeless individuals during crisis situations.

Educational Support for Orphaned Children

Offering scholarships, tutoring programs, and mentorship
opportunities to orphaned children to ensure they have access
to quality education.

Skills Development Workshops

Organizing workshops and training sessions to equip vulnerable
populations with essential skills for employment and self-sustainability.

Volunteer and Support Opportunities

We encourage individuals, businesses, and community
groups to join us in our efforts to make a positive impact.
